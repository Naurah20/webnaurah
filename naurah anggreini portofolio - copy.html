    Art Deco Architecture: Wisma Atlet & Stadium Design

    In the fifth semester, I worked on the design of a Stadium and Athlete’s Village with an Art Deco concept. This concept was chosen to create a structure that is not only functional but also aesthetically appealing, featuring distinctive decorative elements, bold geometric lines, and strong symmetry. The design accommodates the needs of athletes while considering comfort, natural lighting, and optimal air circulation.

    Student Dormitory

    This project is a group assignment, where I was responsible for designing the dormitory for vocational high school students. The dormitory adopts a Modern Tropical concept, characterized by optimal natural lighting, good air circulation through large openings, and shading elements that protect from excessive heat. This design aims to create a comfortable, energy-efficient living environment that aligns with the tropical climate.
